Type 2 Diabetes

Metformin

Dosage: Typically 500-2000 mg daily in divided doses.
Side Effects: Nausea, upset stomach, diarrhea.
Precautions: Not suitable for patients with kidney problems; take with food.

Glipizide

Dosage: Commonly 5-10 mg daily before meals.
Side Effects: Low blood sugar, dizziness, weight gain.
Precautions: Monitor blood sugar levels regularly; caution with alcohol.

Sitagliptin

Dosage: Usually 100 mg once daily.
Side Effects: Upper respiratory infections, headache, nausea.
Precautions: Not recommended in patients with severe kidney disease.
