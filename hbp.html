Hypertension (High Blood Pressure)

Amlodipine

Dosage: Typically 5-10 mg once daily.
Side Effects: Dizziness, swelling in ankles/feet, fatigue.
Precautions: Monitor in patients with liver disease; avoid alcohol.

Lisinopril

Dosage: Usually 10-40 mg daily, depending on severity.
Side Effects: Dry cough, headache, increased potassium levels.
Precautions: Avoid in pregnancy; may interact with potassium supplements.

Losartan

Dosage: Commonly 25-100 mg daily in one or two doses.
Side Effects: Dizziness, nasal congestion, muscle cramps.
Precautions: Avoid in patients with kidney issues; may increase potassium.
